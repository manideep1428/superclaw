## Getting a Telegram Bot Token

To connect Superclae to Telegram, you need to create a dedicated bot via Telegram's official **BotFather**.

### Step-by-Step Guide

1. Open Telegram on your phone or desktop.
2. Search for `@BotFather` in the search bar. This is the official bot for managing other bots.
3. Click **Start** to begin talking to BotFather.
4. Send the command `/newbot`.
5. BotFather will ask you for a **name** for your bot. This is the display name users will see (e.g., "My Superclae Bot").
6. BotFather will ask you for a **username** for your bot. This must end in `bot` (e.g., `SuperClawAI_bot`).
7. Once completed, BotFather will give you a **Bot Token**. It looks something like this:
   `1234567890:ABCdefGHIjklMNOpqrSTUvwxYZ`

Copy this entire token, go back to Superclae's Extension Marketplace, and paste it into the **Bot Token** field!
