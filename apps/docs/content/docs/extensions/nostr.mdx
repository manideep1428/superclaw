# Nostr Extension

Connect your AI agent to Nostr. The agent will sign in as an active Nostr profile, enabling it to receive and respond to decentralized, end-to-end encrypted direct messages (NIP-04/NIP-44) from users across the Nostr protocol.

## Setup Guide

To setup this extension, you need a Nostr private key (`nsec`). This acts as both the "username" and "password" for your bot.

### How to get an nsec key for your bot

Because there are no "corporations" on Nostr, creating a bot account is just like creating a normal human account: you generate a cryptographic keypair.

1. **Use a Client or Generator**
   - Head to a web client like `snort.social`, `primal.net`, or use a tool like `nostrtool.com`.
   - Select **Generate New Key** or **Create Account**.

2. **Copy the Private Key**
   - Your private key will begin with `nsec1...`.
   - **Important**: Treat this key like a password. If anyone gets it, they control the bot's identity completely. Keep it safe.

3. **Choose Relays**
   - You will need to specify which Relays the bot should connect to (this is how Nostr routes messages). Usually, default relays like `wss://relay.damus.io`, `wss://nos.lol`, or `wss://relay.primal.net` are highly popular.

### Connecting in SuperClaw

1. In the SuperClaw dashboard, click **Connect** on the Nostr card.
2. Paste the `nsec1...` **Private Key**.
3. Enter your preferred relays as a comma-separated list.
4. Click **Connect**. The bot is online!

*(Note: Never put your personal human nsec into the bot, unless you want the bot to reply to all of your personal DMs. Always generate a dedicated keypair for the bot!)*
