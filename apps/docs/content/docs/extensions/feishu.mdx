# Feishu / Lark Apps

Feishu (Lark) is an enterprise collaboration platform similar to Slack or Microsoft Teams. You can use this to plug your AI into corporate workspaces.

## Requirements

1. **App ID**
2. **App Secret**

Go to [Feishu Open Platform](https://open.feishu.cn/) or Lark Open Platform and click **Create a Custom App**.

Fill out your App info, then go to **Credentials & Basic Info** in the left menu.
Locate your **App ID** and **App Secret**. Paste them into the connection modal.

You should also grab the **Event Subscription** URL from Superclae to paste into your App's console so Superclae receives events when someone texts your bot!
