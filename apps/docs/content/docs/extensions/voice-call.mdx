# Voice Call Extension

This extension turns your SuperClaw AI into a fully autonomous phone agent. Make outbound notifications, carry on two-way spoken conversations, or handle inbound customer service calls. 

Under the hood, this integrates with voice providers like **Twilio** to manage telephony and SIP orchestration.

## Setup Guide for Twilio

To connect your AI, you'll need three things from Twilio: an Account SID, an Auth Token, and a purchased Phone Number.

### 1. Find your Account SID and Auth Token
1. Go to the [Twilio Console](https://console.twilio.com/) and Log In to your account.
2. Ensure you are on the main **Dashboard**.
3. Scroll to the **Account Info** section at the bottom.
4. You will see your **Account SID** (a 34-character string starting with `AC...`). Copy this.
5. Below that is the hidden **Auth Token**. Click the "eye" icon or the "Copy" icon to retrieve it. Treat this like a password.

### 2. Get a Phone Number
1. Head to **Phone Numbers** -> **Manage** -> **Buy a number**.
2. Purchase a number capable of "Voice".
3. Write down this number in E.164 format (e.g. `+15551234567`). This will be the bot's caller ID.

### 3. Connect to SuperClaw
1. Open the SuperClaw Dashboard and select the **Voice Call** extension.
2. Under "Provider", leave it as `twillio`.
3. Fill in the **Twilio Account SID** and **Twilio Auth Token**.
4. Set the **From Phone Number** to the Twilio number you just purchased.
5. Click Connect!

Your AI can now be programmed via logic chains to call users, or you can route incoming calls from Twilio directly to your SuperClaw `api/voice/incoming` webhook route!
