# WhatsApp Extension

The WhatsApp extension allows your AI assistant to send and receive messages directly through your personal or business WhatsApp account. 

Unlike the official Meta Cloud API which requires complex business verification, this extension uses a "WhatsApp Web" session approach â€” meaning you just scan a QR code to link it.

## Setup Guide

There is no configuration required!

1. Go to your Superclae dashboard and click **Connect** on the WhatsApp card.
2. The modal will load and display a QR code.
3. Open **WhatsApp** on your phone.
4. Go to **Settings** -> **Linked Devices** -> **Link a Device**.
5. Point your phone's camera at the QR code on your screen.
6. Once scanned, the modal will update to say "Connected" and your AI is now live on that WhatsApp account!

## How it works

Behind the scenes, Superclae spins up an isolated, headless WhatsApp Web instance for your account. When messages arrive on your phone, they are instantly forwarded to your AI agent. When your AI replies, it sends the message through that same web session.

**Note:** If your Superclae server restarts, the connection will persist (the session tokens are saved securely), so you usually only have to scan the QR code once. If your phone disconnects the device from WhatsApp settings, you will need to scan it again.
