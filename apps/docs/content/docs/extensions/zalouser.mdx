# Zalo User Extension

Connect Superclae to your personal Zalo account (instead of an Official Account). This operates via the browser-based Zalo Web interface, acting as a headless client to reply to individual personal chats and group mentions.

*Disclaimer: Automating personal accounts violates Zalo Terms of Service and could result in bans. Use an experimental auxiliary phone number if testing this.*

## Setup Guide

Superclae requires extracting your active Zalo Web browser cookie to hijack the session.

### 1. Extract your Zalo Web Cookie
1. Log in to your personal Zalo account via [chat.zalo.me](https://chat.zalo.me/) passing QR or password validation.
2. Once logged in, right-click anywhere on the page and click **Inspect** to open Developer Tools.
3. Select the **Network** tab.
4. Refresh the page (F5 or CMD+R).
5. Click on any large request to `chat.zalo.me/api/...`.
6. Scroll down to the **Request Headers** section and look for `cookie:`.
7. **Copy the entire extremely long string** following `cookie: `.

### 2. Connect to Superclae
1. In the Superclae dashboard, click **Connect** for the Zalo User extension.
2. Paste the **Zalo Web Cookie**.
3. Click Connect! The bot will now operate as your personal account from the IP Superclae is hosted on.
