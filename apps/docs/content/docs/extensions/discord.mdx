## Connecting Discord to SuperClaw

To use Discord, you need to create an application in the Discord Developer Portal.

### 1. Create the Application
1. Go to the [Discord Developer Portal](https://discord.com/developers/applications).
2. Click **New Application** in the top right.
3. Give your bot a name and agree to the Terms of Service. Click **Create**.
4. In the General Information tab, copy the **Application ID**. You will need this for SuperClaw.
5. In the same tab, copy the **Public Key**. You will also need this for SuperClaw.

### 2. Get the Bot Token
1. On the left sidebar, click on **Bot**.
2. Under the username, click **Reset Token**.
3. Discord will generate a new secure token for your bot. **Copy this immediately**. It is your **Bot Token**.

### 3. Setup Permissions
1. On the left sidebar, click **Installation**.
2. Under "Install Link", choose **Discord Provided Link**.
3. Under "Default Install Settings" → "Guild Install" → Scopes, select `bot` and `applications.commands`.
4. Under "Permissions", select **Send Messages** and other required messaging permissions you want for your bot.
5. Save changes. You can use the provided link to invite your bot to a server.

Paste the **Application ID**, **Public Key**, and **Bot Token** into SuperClaw!
