# Synology Chat Extension

Turn your Synology NAS into your own private AI hub by connecting Superclae to Synology Chat. 

## Setup Guide

Superclae requires webhooks configured in your Synology Chat interface.

### 1. Generate Webhooks in Synology Chat
1. Open **Synology Chat** on your NAS.
2. Click on your **User Profile** (top right) -> **Integration**.
3. **Incoming Webhook**: Click `Incoming Webhooks` -> `Create`. 
   - Assign it to a channel or user.
   - Copy the generated **Webhook URL**. It will look like `http://NAS-IP:5000/webapi/entry.cgi?api=SYNO.Chat.External...`
4. **Outgoing Webhook**: Click `Outgoing Webhooks` -> `Create`.
   - Set the Trigger words or assign it to all messages in a specific channel.
   - Point its URL to your Superclae incoming webhook route (e.g., `http://YOUR-Superclae-IP:3000/api/webhook/synology-chat`).
   - Copy the generated **Secret Token**.

### 2. Connect to Superclae
1. In the Superclae dashboard, click **Connect** for Synology Chat.
2. Paste the **Incoming Webhook URL**.
3. Paste the **Outgoing Token** (Secret Token).
4. Click Connect! Your AI is now actively listening and responding inside your NAS chat instances.
